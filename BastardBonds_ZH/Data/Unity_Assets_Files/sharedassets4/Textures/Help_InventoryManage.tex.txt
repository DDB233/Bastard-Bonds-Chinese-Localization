Click or right-click on any of your band members' names to access

their inventory and equipment directly.

Every member of your band will keep their own items safe, and you can store a few items here without worry, but if you'd like to really stock up, save items for crafting, or

trade unwanted items for coin, you should kire a Quartermaster.

There's no telling if your quartermaster will be able to find buyers for the items you put up for sale, and different tupes of items may be harder to sell. An Appraiser can help

items sell more quickly and for more money!

If you ever lose your Quartermaster, the items you have in storage will still be there, but you won't have access to them until you hire another Quartermaster to sort everything out.

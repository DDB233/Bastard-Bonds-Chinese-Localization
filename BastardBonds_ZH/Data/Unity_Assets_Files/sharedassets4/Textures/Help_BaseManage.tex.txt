This is where you can recruit noncombatants for your stronghold.

You require both an available sleeping space and an amount of surplus manpower to bring someone in. If you don't see any options available yet, check back once you've increased

your available manpower.

Everybody in your band contributes to your stronghold's available manpower, but they

contribute differently based on their alignments.

It takes some time to find appropriate people to provide services, so you'll need to come

back at a later time to see if anybody has arrived.
